Study for Biosensing

This paper reported a comparative study of the full gate and short gate Schottky Barrier (SB) Tunnel FET (TFET).

In this study, we introduce a Short Gate Dielectric Modulated Dopant Segregated SB TFET (SG DM DS SB TFET), featuring a short gate architecture with gatedrain underlap and dopant-segregated source/drain (S/D). This design effectively reduces gate control over the drain side, leading to a decrease in leakage current due to ambipolar conduction. The device demonstrates enhanced performance as a label-free biosensor, benefiting from advantages such as freedom from random dopant fluctuations, low thermal budget, reduced device variability, and ease of fabrication.
